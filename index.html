Web Dev | Data Analyst | Social Media Manager

I craft captivating websites, optimized with data and bonus marketing strategies, that will help your brand fly to success.

Welcome to my digital playground, where creativity meets strategy and pixels come alive. I'm a passionate web developer, data analytics whiz, and social media maestro, on a mission to help brands like yours shine online.

Imagine:
  • Websites that captivate visitors, with stunning visuals, intuitive interfaces, and user journeys so smooth, they feel like magic.
  • Data-driven insights that unveil the hidden gems within your audience, informing every tweak and pivot for maximum impact.
  • Social media campaigns that ignite conversations, build communities, and turn casual scrollers into brand advocates.
That's the power I bring to the table.

Let's dive deeper:
  • Web Developer: I craft pixel-perfect websites that are not just beautiful, but strategically designed to convert visitors into loyal customers. From sleek landing pages to user-centric e-commerce experiences, I make sure your online presence is a magnetic force.
  • Data Analyst:Data is my kryptonite. I wield GA4 like a Jedi master, unlocking the secrets of your audience behavior, tracking campaign performance, and optimizing your every move for maximum ROI.
  • Social Media Management:I'm the Pied Piper of the digital town square. I craft engaging content, build thriving communities, and run targeted campaigns that turn likes and shares into real-world results.

Ready to take your brand to the next level? Let's chat! I'm eager to hear your story and craft a digital strategy that makes your online presence impossible to ignore.

P.S. I'm also a big fan of puns, chai lattes, and my toxic trait is I DIY everything I can in our house. Let's talk tech and beyond!
