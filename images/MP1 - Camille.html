Agimat At Ugat Foraging Bar And Kitchen is voted one of the best places for a cocktail in Manila by Time Out Magazine in 2021. Situated in the heart of Poblacion in Makati with two floors of pure art and atmosphere. "Agimat' opens its doors at 5 pm until 2 am with themed music, "on fire" cocktails and excotic food.
